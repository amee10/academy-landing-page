Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior coding experience?

No, this workshop is beginner-friendly. We'll start from the basics and gradually move to advanced concepts. Our step-by-step approach ensures everyone can follow along.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes, all participants will receive a digital certificate upon completion. The certificate is industry-recognized and can be added to your LinkedIn profile and resume.

Is this workshop live?

Yes, it's a fully interactive live session where you can ask questions, participate in discussions, and get real-time feedback from the instructor.

How long is the workshop?

The workshop runs for a full day, divided into four comprehensive sessions with breaks in between. You'll also get lifetime access to the recorded sessions.

What do I need to prepare?

You'll need a laptop with a modern web browser and stable internet connection. We'll provide detailed setup instructions before the workshop.
